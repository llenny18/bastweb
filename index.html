Batangas Governor Mandanas, Atty. Angelica Chua, ikinasal na; Palitan ng Wedding Vows ng newly-wed couple, nakaantig sa marami

“..Mahal, I came here not only with own free will, but full of love. Love which I know is a blessing from our Lord. That we are allowed permitted, granted…to share and to participate. Because God is Love and Love is God.”
